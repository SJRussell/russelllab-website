We welcome enquiries from prospective graduate students, undergraduate researchers, and potential collaborators.

Interested in joining the lab?

We are actively recruiting MSc and undergraduate research students with interests in computational biology, reproductive science, bioinformatics, or multi-omics data analysis. Prior experience in molecular biology or programming (R/Python) is an asset but not required — we value curiosity and determination above all.

If you are a potential collaborator or have a question about our research, we'd love to hear from you too.

What We Look For

We welcome students from diverse backgrounds. No two paths into reproductive biology are the same — if you are excited about the science, we want to talk.

🔬
Scientific Curiosity

A genuine interest in how life begins and what can go wrong — regardless of prior experience in the field.

💻
Computational Interest

Experience or eagerness to learn R, Python, or bioinformatics pipelines. We will train the right person.

🤝
Collaborative Spirit

We are a small, collegial lab. Teamwork, communication, and openness to feedback are essential.
